Medium NEET 2023
In mitosis, the chromosomes reach the poles and start decondensing. Which phase of cell division is this?
A
Metaphase
B
Anaphase
C
Telophase
D
Prophase
✅ Correct Answer: Telophase

During Telophase, chromosomes reach the poles and begin to decondense back into chromatin. The nuclear envelope reforms, nucleoli reappear, and cytokinesis begins. This marks the end of karyokinesis.
Hard NEET 2022
The velocity of a particle moving in the positive direction of x-axis varies as v = α√x, where α is a positive constant. Assuming that at moment t=0 the particle was located at x=0, find the acceleration of the particle.
A
α²/4
B
α²
C
α²/2
D
2α²
✅ Correct Answer: α²/2

v = α√x → a = v·(dv/dx) = α√x · (α/2√x) = α²/2. This is a constant acceleration, independent of position.
